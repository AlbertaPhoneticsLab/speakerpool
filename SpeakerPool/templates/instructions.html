Recording Instructions

A text prompt will appear automatically once you press the button to proceed.

When you press the button, the recording will begin.

In order to stop the recording, press the button.

Once you stop the recording, you can play back what you have recorded using the audio toolbar. If you would like to re-record, you may do so with the button.

Once you are satisfied with the recording, you can submit it by pressing the button.

The recording will be uploaded to the server and the prompt will change automatically.

If at any time a text prompt makes you uncomfortable, you don't know how to pronounce a word, or you don't want to record the prompt for any other reason, you may skip the prompt with the button.

You may stop recording or take a break at any time.
